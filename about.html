ABOUT US

Arm's length is a none profit charitable organization which teachers life skills to children in age ranging from 13years to 23years at a very low fee or for free to those who cannot afford it.

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Arm's lenght believes that all kids deserve a successful and happy future which is obtained from health, caring and happy childhood in addition to life skills training.

OUR DREAM

Our dream is to maintain a happy, safe, healthy and educated life styles for the future generation which are the current teenegars and younger adults.
