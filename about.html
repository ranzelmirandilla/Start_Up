ABOUT US

Arm's length is a none profit charitable organization which teachers life skills to people age ranging from 13years to 23years.

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

The organization committed to ensure that all young adults and teenagers at risk have access to a one on one proffessional mentor to train them on the essentional life skills.

OUR DREAM

Our dream is to maintain a happy, safe, healthy and educated life styles for the future generation which are the current teenegars and younger adults.
