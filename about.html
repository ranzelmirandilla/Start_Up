ABOUT US

Arm's length is a non profit charitable organization which teaches life skills to teens and young adults with the main focus on "Kids which are at risk".We offer training at a very low price and free to those who cannot afford it.

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Arm's length believes that every human being deserves a successful and happy future which is obtained from a health, caring and happy childhood in addition to a professional life skills training for those teens and younger adults who don't have committed and loving parents.

ARM'S LENGTH HAS THREE DREAMS

First dream is to ensure that the future generation which are the current teens and younger adults have an everlasting happy, safe, healthy and educated life.

Second dream is to ensure that all Arm's Length students are trained to eat nutritious meals and practice socialization skills.

The third dream is to ensure that all teens and young adults have mentors who love them unconditionaly and always encourage them do what it takes to achieve their dreams.

OWNERS

Arm's Length was started by two families who realized that one of the way to save the future generation was to take care of the present teens and young adults. So, after years of work experiences these nice wealthy families then decided to retire from their companies and started this non profit Charitable organization.

James

He is the director of the Cooking program. James was a Chef for 10 years before joining Arm's Length.

Mary

Mary is the director of the Budgeting program.She was a Bank manager for 15 years before joining Arm's Length

Peter

Peter was the president of a local community college for 20 years.He is the director of Time Management program at Arm's Length

Rita

Rita came to Arm's Length with 18 years experience in youth programs. She is a director of Community Service program

STAFF

There are four staff members at Arm's Length.Their roll is to help in mentoring the students.They all have Bachelor degrees in human services(teaching, psychology) and 3 years past related work experiences.
