FUNDRAISING PAGE

90% of the funds Arm's Length uses to run it's orgination comes from four fundraisings which are conducted every 1st Saturday of the following months: February,September,November and December. These months where picked because of the major holidays they have and major events in them namely: Valentine, Beginning of Academic year(for September), Thanksgiving and Christmas.We thought people will need to buy gifts or extra money for going back to school around those months. 99% of these items are donated by caring people. Since cars are very expensive, we use some of the organization's money for the months of July and August to buy a brand new car at 85% discount(again we are blessed to have friendly car dealers). Auction for the car is always 1st saturday of September.
