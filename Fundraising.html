funds

90% of the funding Arm's Length has is obtained from four hunge fundraisings which are done on the 1st saturday for month of February,September,November and December. These months where picked because of the major holidays they have and major events in them namely: Valentine, Beginning of Academic year(for September), Thanksgiving and Christmas.We thought people will need to buy gifts or extra money for going back to school around those months. 99% of these items are donated by caring people. Since cars are very expensive, we use some of the organization's money for the months of July and August to buy a brand new car at 85% discount(again we are blessed to have friendly car dealers). Auction for the car is always 1st saturday of September.
