Arm's Length Store

All shop proceeds go directly back to the Arm's Length program to help fund new classes, buy materials and sponsor students who need extra help.
We have some classic designs that will always be avaiable for purchase, and we also have rotating designs from some of our artisically inclined members- so be sure to check out the store for seasonal offerings! Artwork made by our students is also avaiable for purchase- it can be found in the lobby of our downtown Albany location. For details, please contact us.

In addition, we have two long standing programs to help some of the most vulnlerable in our community during the harshest weather conditions, Buy a Hoodie, Give Some Warmth and Share the H20.

Buy a Hoodie, Give Some Warmth with each purchase of a hooded sweatshirt, a sweatshirt, jacket or coat goes to a young person in need during the coldest time of the year

Share the H20 the purchases of water bottles and coffee tumblrs help support our programs to provide clean water to young people in need
